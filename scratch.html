The Unthinkable Becomes Reality

Chevrolet has shattered expectations with the 2025 Corvette ZR1. By taking the flat-plane crank V8 from the Z06 and bolting on two massive turbochargers, the engineers in Bowling Green have created an absolute monster that rivals the most exclusive hypercars from Europe.

The Anatomy of a Hypercar

What makes the new ZR1 so special? It's not just the engine; it's a complete aerodynamic and mechanical overhaul:

  1. The LT7 Engine:
    • 5.5L Twin-Turbocharged Flat-Plane Crank V8
    • 1,064 Horsepower @ 7,000 RPM
    • 828 lb-ft of Torque
    • First factory turbocharged Corvette in history
  2. ZTK Performance Package: An aggressive aero kit generating over 1,200 lbs of downforce at top speed.
  3. The Split Window Returns: A gorgeous homage to the legendary 1963 Corvette, built into the carbon fiber rear hatch to help vent engine heat.
  4. Carbon Fiber Everywhere: Even the wheels are available in full carbon fiber to reduce unsprung mass.
